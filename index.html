Profile overview

I am a theoretical and computational physicist working at the Catholic University of Eastern Africa, and also an affiliated researcher at the Technical University of Kenya. I use various theoretical and simulation methods to study materials properties. Most of my work involves applications of Density Functional Theory, an approach that offers unique insight into the microscopic origin of diverse phenomena, as well as the predictive power necessary for designing novel systems optimized for applications. My current research focuses on functional oxides, especially ferroelectrics and magnetoelectric multiferroics.

I am a physicist, a software developer, a web designer & administrator, a data analyst, and equally a historian.
